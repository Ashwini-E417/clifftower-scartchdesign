Architect

Sanjay Puri is one of India's most celebrated architects on the international stage, with an engagement of more than 360 awards from global platforms such as the World Architecture Festival, LEAF (UK), and Architizer A+ Awards. Sanjay Puri Architects experiment with bold geometries and climate sensitivity, juxtaposing sculptural form with practical solutions.
